Transfer Credits

How transfer credits get awarded

When students fill out a transfer application, transcripts from previous schools are required from the student by the University Application Processing Center (UAPC). If you are transferring from a CUNY school, you do not have to mail your transcript to BMCC. The exception to this is when there is a financial obligation from the student by the previous school. Transcripts will not be released until the financial obligation is resolved. When BMCC receives your application, your transcript is included.

Students who do not provide UAPC with an official transcript, will be required to have an official transcript sent to BMCC for evaluation. Please note, transfer credits will not be awarded until the official transcript is received.

What is Transfer Credit?

If you passed a class at an accredited college before coming to BMCC you may be able to transfer those credits to BMCC. If your transfer credits are accepted BMCC will identify an equal class at BMCC and give you credit as having taken that class. It will appear on documents listing your courses taken with a TR (for transfer) in place of a grade.

How does Transfer Credit Help You?

If your transfer class is one of the classes required by your BMCC major then you will not have to take that class at BMCC. This may help you graduate faster and with less cost.
