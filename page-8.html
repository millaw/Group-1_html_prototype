Register for Classes

After you have gone through orientation and have been advised, you will be ready to register for classes. Here are some quick steps on how to register for classes.

  1. Login to CUNYfirst at: home.cunyfirst.cuny.edu
  2. Once you log in, follow the navigation below to begin adding classes to your shopping cart.
    HR/Campus Solutions ➔ Self Service ➔ Student Center
  3. In the Academics Sections, click the Enroll link and Select appropriate term (e.g., Fall 2015) and select Continue.
  4. Click the green button under Find Classes to search through the schedule of classes.
  5. Under Search Criteria, select at least 2 search fields (subject, course number). To further refine your search, select Additional Search Criteria. Select

  7. Review the class details and select Next. The class has now been added to your Shopping Cart.
    8. ***IMPORTANT NOTE*** Adding classes to your shopping cart does not confirm enrollment.

  10. Continue Steps 4-8 until you have enrolled in all of your classes.
