Bursar / Financial Aid

***You must first register for class before you get your bill.
Paying your Bill

You may finalize your bill in one or more of the following ways:

  • Paying tuition and fees in full with Financial Aid. If your financial aid grants and loans appear on your account as pending aid or anticipated aid, no further action with the Bursar is required; your bill is automatically considered finalized.
  • Pay tuition and fees online with credit card or electronic check transfer. Tuition plans are available fall and spring only.
  • By a Third Party Voucher from your employer. Obtain a voucher from your employer and present it at the time of registration to defer your tuition. Third party vouchers are applicable only when the college is authorized to directly bill the company. Employee reimbursement plans are not considered third party.
  • By obtaining a Veteran's Deferral. In order to ensure that class reservations for the semester, try to complete your registration and bill payment as soon as you can but no later than close of business on assigned date

Paying with Financial Aid:
Log in to CUNYfirst at www.bmcc.cuny.edu/cunyfirst/student.jsp

Once a student logs in and follows the navigation on CUNYfirst page they can proceed as needed.
The HR/Campus Solutions ➔ Self Service ➔ Account Inquiry link is seen.

If a student needs to make a payment because they do not have pending financial aid to cover the full tuition, they may select - Make a Payment.

Enter the dollar amount in the payment amount column. (Payment should be made in full.) Always remember to select next after making a selection.

  • Select your payment method - credit card or electronic check.
  • Enter payment details (credit card information, etc.).
  • Confirm payment and submit.
